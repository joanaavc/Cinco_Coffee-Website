# Cinco Coffee Website

## Project Overview
This website was developed by BSIS 3rd year students from the Technological University of the Philippines as part of their CC312-M Web Development course. It showcases Cinco Coffee, a mobile pop-up coffee business offering premium coffee and non-coffee beverages through online orders and pop-up stalls.

## Purpose
To establish Cinco Coffee's digital presence, enable e-commerce functionality for online orders, showcase the complete beverage catalog, and foster customer engagement through direct communication channels. The website serves as a central hub for brand storytelling, product information, promotional announcements, and order processing.

## Site Structure
- `index.html` – Homepage with hero section, brand story, and product slider
- `about.html` – Company profile with mission, vision, and founding story
- `productupdate.html` – Complete beverage catalog with shopping cart functionality
- `announcements.html` – Promotional offers and pop-up store locations
- `contact.html` – Contact information, business hours, and feedback form
- `checkout.html` – Order processing with customer information and payment methods
- `cincostyles.css` – Main stylesheet with responsive design
- `assets/` – Product images and brand visuals

## Design Concept
- **Color Scheme**: Coral/burnt orange (#D4764E) primary brand color, cream/beige (#F5EFE7) secondary background, deep brown accents for typography
- **Typography**: Playfair Display (serif) for headings, Poppins (sans-serif) for body text
- **Layout**: Mobile-first responsive design with consistent spacing, WCAG AA accessibility compliance, semantic HTML structure

## Features
- **E-Commerce Functionality**: Full shopping cart system with add/remove items, quantity controls, and localStorage persistence
- **Payment Options**: Cash on Delivery, GCash, and Credit/Debit Card processing
- **Product Catalog**: 7 coffee-based drinks and 5 non-coffee drinks with size options (350ml and 16oz)
- **Responsive Design**: Optimized for mobile, tablet, and desktop devices
- **Accessibility**: Semantic HTML, proper color contrast, keyboard navigation support
- **Order Management**: Automated order number generation and confirmation system
